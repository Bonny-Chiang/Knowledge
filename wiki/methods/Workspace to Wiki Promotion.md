# Workspace to Wiki Promotion

Project work starts in `workspace/`. Only stable knowledge should move into `wiki/`.

## Promote

- reusable concepts
- validated methods
- settled decisions
- durable project summaries
- final reports or distilled lessons

## Do Not Promote

- scratch notes
- temporary prompts
- failed experiments unless they teach a reusable lesson
- raw logs
- intermediate outputs
